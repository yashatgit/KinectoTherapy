KinectoTherapy

A physical rehabilitation tool which makes rehabilitation process fun, motivating and rewarding using Motion-Sensing games.
Features
Full Body Tracking
Multiple Natural User Interfaces (NUIs) to help the user.
Mini Games
Bring some fun element in your monotonous and repetitive exercise!
For all age groups
Customise the software and change difficulty level according to your need.
Feedback Mechanism
Get run time updates about your performance. Detailed report on completion of exercise routine.
Windows / OSX
Be platform independent. Same software runs on Mac and PC.
Data Logging
Play games and get a graphical report about how you performed. Lets your Doctor check the improvement comparing the previous attempts.
No sensors attached to the body
You are the controller. No external wires and machines inhibiting your movement.
Customizable
Customise the application according to your needs. Change difficulty levels!
